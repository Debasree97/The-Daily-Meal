Popular Recipes

Here is some popular recipes that people absolutely love! Make sure to try at home.

Rui Macher Jhaal

Bengali cuisine is just flooded with macher (fish) varieties. Of all the dishes that you have and have not heard of, there are some unique varieties that are very simple yet delicious. One of those is "Kacha Tomato diye Macher Jhaal" The dish uses unripe tomato to add tanginess and is absolutely delectable.

Aamer Tok Dal

Honestly speaking, when raw mango mingles with red lentil, wonder happens. Tok Dal is when you cook typical Bengali-style lentil soup, but, add some raw mango pieces for that tanginess and yumminess. This is a summer delight. Bengali cuisine has some super interesting ways to use raw mangoes in many dishes.

Fulkopi Roast

"Fulkopi Roast" is one of the most amazing Bengali winter delicacies. While seasonal veggies are powerhouses of nutrients, they also are delicious and used for tons of seasonal dishes. This a semi-dry dish and is packed with flavours. This is a mildly spiced dish and goes extremely well with "roti" and rice.
